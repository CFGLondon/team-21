The data found on the website is directly sourced by the sufferers of disabilities themselves, which provides a detailed overview of the issues that affect their lives. The methods of data extraction are the following: SMS, a call service that allows people that are illiterate to contribute and an online form. The data is open source so everyone can access it and try to make a difference.
